Inauguration of Smile Kanpur's Happiness School Initiative

We are proud to have celebrated the inauguration of Smile Kanpur's Happiness School Initiative at our institution. This initiative aims to promote emotional well-being, positive education, and holistic development among students. The event featured distinguished guests, engaging activities, and a showcase of how happiness-centered education practices will be integrated into our curriculum.

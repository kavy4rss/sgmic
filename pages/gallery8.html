Discussion on Exams with PM Modi

Our students had the privilege of participating in a nationwide program where the Honorable Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi Ji shared valuable insights on managing exam stress and anxiety. The "Pariksha Pe Charcha" session provided our students with guidance on exam preparation, time management, and maintaining a positive mindset during examinations.

Date: 10 January, 2025

Back to All Categories

Key Takeaways from PM Modi's Session

  • Time Management: Effective strategies to balance study time with breaks and recreation
  • Stress Reduction: Techniques to manage exam anxiety and maintain mental well-being
  • Focus on Learning: Emphasis on understanding concepts rather than rote memorization
  • Parental Support: Guidance for parents on how to create a positive environment for students
  • Technology Balance: Using technology as a tool for learning without getting distracted
  • Positive Mindset: Approaching exams with confidence and the right attitude

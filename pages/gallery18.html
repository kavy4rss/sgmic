Guru Pujan Celebration 2025

Date: 10 July 2025

Guru Pujan was celebrated with great devotion and respect at Saraswati Gyan Mandir Inter College. Students paid tribute to their teachers and gurus through various cultural programs, traditional rituals, and heartfelt performances. The event highlighted the importance of guru-shishya relationship and the role of teachers in shaping young minds.

Namami Gange Awareness Drive

On July 25, 2024, Saraswati Gyan Mandir Inter College organized a special Namami Gange Awareness Drive to educate students about the importance of preserving our national river and its ecosystem. This initiative, aligned with the Government of India's Namami Gange Programme, aimed to create awareness about river conservation, water pollution, and the cultural significance of the Ganga River among our students.

Date: May 3, 2025

Back to All Categories

Key Awareness Initiatives

  • Educational presentations on the significance of the Ganga River ecosystem
  • Interactive sessions on preventing water pollution and waste management
  • Drawing and poster-making competition on river conservation themes
  • Pledge-taking ceremony to protect water bodies and reduce plastic usage
  • Documentary screening about the Namami Gange Programme's achievements
  • Distribution of informational pamphlets to spread awareness in the community

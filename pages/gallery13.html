Health Awareness and Vaccination Program

In collaboration with local health authorities, our school organized a comprehensive Health Awareness and Vaccination Program for students. The initiative aimed to educate students about preventive healthcare, proper hygiene practices, and provide essential vaccinations. Medical professionals conducted health check-ups and delivered informative talks on maintaining good health and preventing common diseases.

Program Benefits

  • Comprehensive health check-ups for early detection of health issues
  • Age-appropriate vaccinations to protect against preventable diseases
  • Interactive sessions on personal hygiene and preventive healthcare
  • Nutritional guidance for students and parents
  • Distribution of health kits containing essential hygiene products
  • Awareness about physical fitness and mental wellbeing

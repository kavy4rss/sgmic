A Special Day of Fun and Film

On July 15, 2024, our students enjoyed a memorable day filled with fun activities and an educational film screening. This special event combined entertainment with learning, providing students an opportunity to relax while gaining valuable insights from carefully selected films that aligned with their curriculum and developmental needs.

Event Highlights

  • Screening of educational and inspirational films suitable for different age groups
  • Interactive discussions following the film screenings to reflect on key messages
  • Fun activities and games related to the film themes
  • Refreshments and snacks during intermissions
  • Special appearance by local filmmakers who shared insights about filmmaking
  • Creative expression session where students shared their takeaways through art

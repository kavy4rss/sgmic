Earth Day Celebration

Our school community came together to celebrate Earth Day with a series of engaging activities focused on environmental awareness and sustainability. Students participated in tree plantations, recycling projects, educational presentations, and artistic displays promoting eco-friendly practices. Through these activities, our students learned about their role in protecting our planet for future generations.

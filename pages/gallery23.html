15 August 2025

Viksit Bharat, Ek Saath

India celebrates its 78th Independence Day on August 15, 2025, honoring the sacrifices of freedom fighters and embracing the spirit of unity, progress, and patriotism under the theme "Viksit Bharat, Ek Saath".

From flag hoisting ceremonies and cultural showcases to digital tributes and community events, this national holiday invites every citizen to reflect on the journey of freedom and contribute to a brighter future.

Basant Panchami Celebration

Our school celebrated Basant Panchami with great enthusiasm and devotion. Students and staff came together to worship Goddess Saraswati, the deity of knowledge, music, arts, and learning. The celebration featured traditional yellow attire, cultural performances, and special prayers seeking blessings for academic success.

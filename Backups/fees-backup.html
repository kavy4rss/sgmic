Important Information

Fee Policies

Payment Schedule

Fees are payable quarterly in advance. The quarters are as follows:

  • Quarter 1: April - June
  • Quarter 2: July - September
  • Quarter 3: October - December
  • Quarter 4: January - March

Discounts & Concessions

We offer the following concessions to eligible students:

  • 10% discount on tuition fee for siblings
  • 15% scholarship for meritorious students
  • Special concessions for children of staff members
  • Financial aid for economically disadvantaged students

Late Payment Policy

Fees should be paid by the 10th of the first month of each quarter. Late payment will incur the following penalties:

  • Delay up to 15 days: ₹200 fine
  • Delay up to 30 days: ₹500 fine
  • Delay beyond 30 days: May result in disciplinary action

Refund Policy

In case of withdrawal or transfer:

  • Admission fee is non-refundable
  • Tuition fee for the ongoing quarter is non-refundable
  • Security deposit is refundable after adjusting dues
  • One month notice required for any refund processing

Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any additional fee for school transportation?

Yes, transportation fee is separate from the regular school fee. It varies based on the distance from school to your residence. The fee ranges from ₹1,500 to ₹3,000 per quarter depending on the route. Please contact the transportation department for specific details and route information.

Are there any additional charges for extracurricular activities?

Basic extracurricular activities are covered in the regular fee structure. However, specialized training programs like advanced music, dance, sports coaching, and educational trips may have additional charges. Parents will be informed in advance about any such programs and their associated costs.

Is there any scholarship program available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for academically excellent students, sports scholarships for outstanding athletes, and need-based financial aid for economically disadvantaged students. Scholarship amounts range from 15% to 50% of the tuition fee depending on the category and eligibility criteria. Applications for scholarships are invited at the beginning of the academic year.

What is the policy for fee revision?

The school reviews the fee structure annually before the beginning of each academic year. Any fee revision is communicated to parents at least two months in advance. The revision is typically in line with inflation and increasing operational costs. We strive to keep fee hikes reasonable and justified.

Can I pay the whole year's fee in advance?

Yes, parents can choose to pay the entire year's fee in advance. In fact, we offer a 5% discount on the total tuition fee amount for annual payments made at the beginning of the academic year. This option is available during the first quarter payment window.

Is there any additional fee for board examination?

Yes, for classes 10 and 12, there are additional examination fees as prescribed by the UP Board. These fees are collected separately approximately three months before the board examinations. The exact amount is communicated based on the current UP Board fee structure for that particular academic year.

Need More Information?

If you have any questions regarding our fee structure or payment process, please don't hesitate to contact our accounts department. We're here to help you understand our fee policies better.

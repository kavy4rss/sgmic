Saraswati Gyan Mandir
Inter College Azad Nagar Kanpur

All human life is education and education is life.

We believe that value-based education is the true education. Unfortunately, in today's educational landscape, there is a significant neglect of values and cultural traditions. This lack of moral, ethical, and spiritual dimensions has led to instability, unrest, exploitation, oppression, and corruption in our society. Despite progress in many areas that has brought recognition to our nation, the developmental outcomes we expected over this extended period have not been fully realized. Children remain at the center of our hope for a better future.

Education and values are the birthright of every child. Our ancestors recognized this right and established systems for imparting both knowledge and values through the principle of "Mata Guru" (Mother is the first teacher). We consider this a collective social responsibility to uphold and continue this tradition.

Academic Year 2024-25

UP Board Curriculum & Syllabus

Our curriculum follows the guidelines prescribed by the Uttar Pradesh Board of High School and Intermediate Education. We offer classes from 1st to 8th standard. The syllabus is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of subjects while promoting critical thinking, creativity, and practical application of knowledge.
